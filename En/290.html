Audi Mexico's plant will stop production until January

Audi Mexico is stopping production until January, the general secretary of workers confirmed that this is a measure taken because of adjustments to the production lines before a lower unit demand. This will occur through a combined cycle of vacations and technical shutdowns. This obeys an adjustment in the production calendar before a lower unit demand in the worldwide markets and a negative numbers outlook for the global automotive industry.

Queretaro hopes that the USMCA's ratification will reactivate automotive investment

Before the ratification of the United States–Mexico–Canada Agreement (USMCA), the local automotive industry foresees that investment projects that were awaiting the application of the trade agreement will be reactivated. The director of the Automotive Cluster of Queretaro, Daniel Hernandez, explained that there's at least four detected investment projects by the cluster that could reactivate their growth plans in the state, these projects would mainly be expansions. He also added that there's a projected increase in the arrival of investment projects with Asian capital.

Honda Mexico occupies the sixth place in the national market

Between January and November of this year, Honda sold 66,318 vehicles in Mexico, which gave the brand 5.6% market participation, or sixth place, according to data from the National Institute of Statistics and Geography (INEGI), highlighted in the internal automotive market report from the National Automotive Dealer Association (AMDA). In the SUV segment, there's three of the brand's models topping the list of the most sold vehicles: CR-V in first place with 18,117 sols units; HR-V in third place with 15,256 sold units and CX-5 with 13,239.

There's a decrease in the number of vehicles acquired through credit

Between January and October of 2019 there were 724,867 vehicles sold through credit in Mexico, 7.6% less than the amount registered in the same period of last year, which meant the lowest sales volume using financing in four years. From the total number of cars sold using a loan from a financial institution, 12% corresponds to used cars, this is the highest percentage registered by the sector in at least five years. Brand's financial arms represent a total of 66.1% of the total units sold through credit, this is less than the 68.7% from the first 10 months of 2018.

ULSD guaranteed in the metropolitan areas of Mexico City, Guadalajara and Monterrey

Mexico City's head of government, Claudia Sheinbaum, informed that the representatives of Semarnat and CAMe guaranteed the implementation of the new environmental norms and that there will be ultra-low sulfur diesel (ULSD) available in the city's service stations. She also mentioned that there's an agreement between Petroleos Mexicanos; the Ministry of Energy; Semarnat and the governments of the megalopolises of Guadalajara, Monterrey and Mexico City, for the guaranteed supply of ULSD.

Mercedes-Benz Buses mobilizes Aguascalientes with 30 new buses

Mercedes-Benz Buses and its distributor, Euro Centro Camionero, working with the state government's mobility project "YoVoy" kicked off the start of operations of 30 buses, of a total of 70 units, which will be implemented to mobilize 35,000 Aguascalientes citizens daily. The 30 new buses have an engine with BlueTec®5 low emission technology with the EURO V certification, they have an OF 1321/44 y LO 914 chassis from the three star automaker and bodywork from Beccar and AYCO.

30 Volvo buses were incorporated into Mexico City's transportation network

The kickoff that marked the start of circulation of the thirty buses saw the presence of the city's head of government, Claudia Sheinbaum; the minister of Mobility of Mexico City, Andres Lajous; the director of the Passenger Transport Network (RTP) of Mexico City, Ramon Jimenez Lopez, as well as Volvo Buses Mexico's corporate commercial director, Moshé Winer, and Volvo Buses' central zone manager, Carlos Acosta. In this way, there will be almost 200 buses with Volvo technology driving around the capital as part of this network.
