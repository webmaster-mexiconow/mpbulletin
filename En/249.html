Bridgestone has a new manufacturing director for its plant in Morelos

Bridgestone, a world leader in the manufacture of tires and other diversified products, announced the appointment of Edgar Cárdenas Briceño as the new managing director of the company's plant located in the city of Cuernavaca, Morelos. This new assignment seeks to provide dynamism in the leadership of its manufacturing operations in the North Latin American region, while improving integration with the Bridgestone Americans Manufacturing Group throughout Latin America.
The new Hino 500 Serie will be manufactured in Guanajuato

The new Hino 500 Series will be produced with an investment of US$100 million, said Kenji Negakubo, head of design for this platform of the Japanese firm, which is being introduced as part of the work of the XIX National Forum of Transport of Goods, organized by the National Association of Private Transportation (ANTP). The executive said that the redesign of the vehicle's platform favors factors such as efficiency in operation and fuel consumption, performance, safety, as well as comfort for the operator.
Sales of heavy vehicles grew 48.7% in the first half of 2019

The National Association of Producers of Buses, Trucks, and Tractor Trucks (ANPACT) reported that, during the month of June, wholesale sales of heavy and commercial vehicles reached a total of 8,310 units, compared to 3,406 registered in June 2018, representing an increase of 144%. During the same month, retail sales of heavy and commercial vehicles reached 4,161 units, which, compared to those sold during the same period of 2018, showed an increase of 22.7%.
Hybrid and electric sales have increased in the Bajío

In March 2019, the number of hybrid and electric vehicles sold among the Bajío states grew by 100% compared to the same month of the previous year, according to the National Institute of Statistics and Geography (INEGI). In total, the area reported 170 units of this type, which doubles the 85 vehicles that were registered in the third month of 2018, the agency said. According to the institute's data, most of the units sold in the sector correspond to hybrid vehicles.
BMW Group celebrates 25 years of operations in Mexico

Since its arrival in Mexico in 1994, the BMW Group has demonstrated its leadership in the premium segment of the country's automotive industry, thanks to the commitment and confidence that the group has had with the Latin American region. This is reflected through its sold network of distributors and importers, as well as the recent inauguration of its production plant in San Luis Potosí. The Group's confidence and commitment to Mexico have been demonstrated through the commercialization of the products of its three BMW, MINI and BMW Motorrad brands.
BASF inaugurates the expansion of its production plant

As part of the investments that BASF has implemented in North America during the last years, the expansion of the automotive paint plant "Base Agua" was carried out where executives, clients, and collaborators met at the Tultitlán site to cut the ribbon, which symbolizes a new productive stage for BASF in Mexico. "The transformation of our plant will generate significant growth in our division. This is the result of the current performance of the Mexican automotive industry that comprised at the end of last year a production of 3.9 million cars," said Alex Attlesey, vice president of Coatings America.
KIA Motors Mexico plans to increase its exports in 2019

KIA Motors Mexico expects to export close to 240,000 units in 2019, which would represent an annual growth of 8.2% in its sales abroad. In addition, the company is ready to comply with the new rule of origin of the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA), which will help them strengthen its presence in this region of the continent. "We consider to have a strategic plant in the country for the manufacture of compact models, and with that, to supply to the American market and the rest of America. This is the strategy we seek to consolidate," explained Horacio Chávez, managing director of KIA Motors Mexico.
