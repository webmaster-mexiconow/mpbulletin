Aguascalientes' automotive industry attracted US$183 million in FDI

In the second quarter of 2021, Aguascalientes attracted US$183 million in foreign direct investment from the automotive sector, 8.9% of the total amount invested by said industry in the country. According to the analysis of the Automotive Directory, Aguascalientes was the third state in the country with the highest number of investments in the automotive industry, as well as San Luis Potosí, surpassed by Coahuila and Guanajuato...

Guanajuato, leader in automotive production

The Bajío region's leadership in automotive production is currently undisputed. During the first six months of the year, the region, with 9 light vehicle assembly plants in Guanajuato, Aguascalientes and San Luis Potosí, has produced more than 750,000 cars, from compacts to pickups. This represents 47.36% of all vehicles produced during the first half of 2021 in Mexico...
