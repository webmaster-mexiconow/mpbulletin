Audi de Mexico, located in San Jose Chiapa, Puebla, will build a Solar Energy Park to supply the automotive plant, according to the Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA). The project consists of an electric power generation plant using photovoltaic conversion. The installed capacity of the Power Plant will have a gross capacity of 5,195 kwp in direct current and a net generation capacity of 4,200 kw in alternating current...

In the accumulated January-May period, the industry reported 15,191 heavy vehicles sold wholesale, a growth of 33.1% compared to the 11,414 units registered in the same period last year...
