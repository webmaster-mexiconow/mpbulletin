Mexico's automotive industry has its worst August in 11 years

In August, the production and export of vehicles in Mexico experienced the greatest reduction since the 2009 crisis, according to data from the National Institute of Statistics and Geography (Inegi). During August of this year, 237,040 cars were assembled in the country, 21.4% less than in the same month of 2020. This is the third consecutive month with falls in production. On the other hand, 212,687 units were exported, 19.6% less than in the same period last year...
