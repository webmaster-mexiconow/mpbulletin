AMIA and AMDA declare a crisis in the Automotive Industry

After 25 months of downside sales, the automotive industry in Mexico was declared in crisis, and it is warned that the picture can be exacerbated if they give the green light to "chocolate" cars. "We must turn on the alarms, because we must reactivate the domestic market," said Eduardo Solis, executive president of the Mexican Association of the Automotive Industry (AMIA).
Continental invests US$584 million in its new plant in Aguascalientes

The construction of the Continental plant began in Aguascalientes, which will be dedicated to the manufacture of electronic components for the automotive industry and will be carried out with an initial investment of US$584 million, allowing the creation of 1,000 high added value jobs in the coming years.

Hyundai Motor of Mexico reaches 21,869 units sold

Hyundai Motor of Mexico reached 21,869 units sold in the first half of the year with its Accent sedan model, which has positioned itself as the favorite vehicle among the firm's clients, reaching 25.45% of the total units placed in the market.
KIA Motors Mexico plans to increase exports in 2019

KIA Motors Mexico expects to export close to 240,000 units in 2019, which would represent an annual growth of 8.2% in its sales abroad. In addition, the company is ready to comply with the new rule of origin of the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA) and strengthen its presence in this region of the continent.
Nissan Maxima 2019 arrives in Mexico

With its high-performance image, 19-inch wheels with diamond cut, and LED headlights in the shape of a boomerang framing the long V-Motion grille, Nissan Maxima 2019 is presented in Mexico with an update to its bold design and new technologies that elevate the premium sensation of this iconic model.
Hino Motors launches a renewed 500 Series

Kenji Nagakubo, Director of Development of Cargo Vehicles of Hino Motors Ltd., along with managers of Hino Mexico, presented the advantages of the new 500 Series that has a redesign with more character, bigger emblem, and futuristic aspect, as well as the new Euro 5 engine with SCR system to comply with NOM-044 for polluting emissions.
