General Motors to halt operations for the third time

The General Motors plant in San Luis Potosí will once again go into technical shutdown due to the semiconductor shortage that has hit the automotive industry worldwide. The company has reported that its supply chain, engineering and manufacturing teams continue to work closely with suppliers to find a solution to mitigate the impact of the chips' absence. Other complexes that will also stop production are General Motors Complejo SLP and General Motors Complejo Ramos Arizpe, each covering the weeks of July 19 and 26...
