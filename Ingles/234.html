OSRAM Continental opens subsidiary in Guadalajara

OSRAM Continental, a global provider of smart automotive lighting solutions, has opened its subsidiary in Guadalajara, which will serve as a research and development (R&D) center to support companies, mainly in the Americas region. The 2,100-square-meter facility will initially employ 65 people and, "is an important addition to our global engineering and design, these are R&D initiatives focused on intelligent automotive lighting solutions for the evolving automotive market," said Dirk Linzmeier, CEO of OSRAM Continental.
Jaguar presents its electric I-Pace model in Mexico City

The Autodromo Hermanos Rodriguez was the perfect setting to welcome the new member of the Jaguar family, the all-new Jaguar I-Pace, the first 100% electric SUV of the prestigious English brand. The launch event was attended by various guests, such as distributors, subsidiary team, clients, influencers and businessmen, who were able to learn about the new vehicle and enjoy an exciting driving experience on the F1 track located in Mexico City.

The largest and most luxurious member of the BMW X family arrives in Mexico: the first BMW X7

The all-new and larger model of the BMW X family reaches Mexican dealerships as of May. The BMW X7 is a vehicle that combines agile and versatile driving with a new sense of space in the luxury segment. Said model is built on the largest plant of the Bavarian firm in Spartanburg, United States, where the BMW X3, BMW X4, BMW X5 and BMW X6 are also produced. The design of the model is characterized by having larger and more harmonious proportions offering 5,151 millimeters in length, 2,000 millimeters in width.
Volkswagen models are exhibited at the Puebla Automobile Museum

Volkswagen Mexico reported that it is holding a special show dedicated to the Sedan and the New Beetle models, which this year finalizes its world production, at the Puebla Automobile Museum (MAP). "Within the framework of the 65th anniversary of the Volkswagen brand in Mexico, we want to share this exhibition that has been specially chosen to celebrate two emblematic models: the Sedan and the New Beetle," said Consuelo Minutti Laso, manager of Corporate Communications at Volkswagen Mexico.
Cooper Standard invests US$15 million in expansion of its Aguascalientes plant

Cooper Standard has invested US$15 million in the expansion of its facilities, a factor that will generate new jobs in the plant of the American company located in the south of the city of Aguascalientes, which already employs 1,350 workers. In an official event, the governor of the state of Aguascalientes, Martin Orozco Sandoval, celebrated the investment and expansion of the plant located in the San Francisco Industrial Park. In addition, the company has been recognized for its socially sustainable projects, as they have promoted public spaces for community development.
