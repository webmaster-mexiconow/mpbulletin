Kia celebrates its fifth anniversary in our country

Kia Motors Mexico arrived to our country in 2015 not only to position itself as a competitor in the highly competitive automotive industry, but also to change the market rules and become one of the leading brands on a national level, a consolidated top 5 member in the sales ranking. It's been 5 years of challenges and learning experiences, of consolidating a distributor network that covers 99% of the country, of releasing 12 competitive and relevant models in their segment. Restricted production in Chihuahua is not sustainable: Ford

Christopher Landau, American ambassador to our country, said that a high executive within Ford told him that the vehicle assembler was worried because of the engines produced in the state of Chihuahua, where the governor has "limited industrial capacity to 50%" due to the coronavirus. Ford later said in a statement, that the restricted production in its Chihuahua plant "is not sustainable" although it added that, with its plants in the United States working at 100%, they don't expect any industrial impacts this week.

JAC Mexico reports growth of 57% in sales during June

At the close of the month of June 2020, JAC Mexico reported a sales growth of 57% when compared to last month. The brand's most sold vehicle was the SUV JAC Sei2, on the other hand, the pickup Frison T8 reported a sales increase of 35%. "We're very happy with the results we've obtained this month. Without a doubt, these have been difficult times but we're keeping firm with the objective of being the brand that recovers the sales rhythm we had in February of this year, and maybe even surpassing it," said Elias Massri, CEO and director of Giant Motors Latin America.
