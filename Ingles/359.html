The CUPRA Ateca Limited Edition arrives to the country with 50 units

The sports division of the Spanish brand SEAT has cut through with a strong brand personality and a range of models that don't stop announcing novelties. GM of Mexico is the automotive company with the best reputation in Merco's rating

General Motors of Mexico was recognized for the 5th consecutive year as the best evaluated automotive company in the ranking "The 100 Companies with the best reputation in Mexico" from Merco (Corporate Reputation Business Monitor). In the eight edition of this ranking, in which 22 sectors were evaluated such as financial, pharmaceutical, IT, food, beverages, insurance, packaging, entertainment and telecommunications among others, GM occupied the 42nd position. Year after year, Merco carries out this evaluation based on a cross-sectional methodology made up by 5 evaluations and 16 information sources.

Pollmann establishes its fifth produtcion hub in the country

Austrian Tier 2 automotive provider, Pollmann, has finally established its fifth international production location in Mexico in the industrial zone between San Miguel de Allende and Queretaro. The project was created to satisfy the demand from Pollmann's clients, who through a press release, informed that they already have orders for door blocking systems for BMW and Daimler at this location. Pollmann will initially have a production area of 5,000 square meters, with the expectation of a sustained growth in the region.

The SE suspends the expedition of origin certificates for exports between Mexico and Brazil

The Ministry of Economy (SE) informed that, starting on August 26, 2020, the expedition of origin certificates for exports established between Mexico and Brazil has been suspended, which is why auto parts destined for the manufacturing of a vehicle assembled in Brazil will not be exported. The two countries hold a commercial agreement similar to the USMCA, which is why it will eventually be necessary to establish an agreement in regards to the reach and expansion of said agreement. Users are reminded that, if the auto parts that they export have a regional content index of 40%, they will be able to proceed with their RPE and go through the habitual procedures.

The Toyota Yaris R says goodbye to Mexico

Toyota and Mazda signed a collaboration agreement in 2014 to jointly manufacture a new model in Mazda's installations located in Salamanca, Guanajuato. A year later, the Toyota Yaris R was born, a sedan that shared everything with the Mazda2, aside from some design adaptations and Toyota's insignia. After half a decade in the market, the Toyota Yaris R is saying goodbye to the Japanese automaker's portfolio in Mexico and the region.

Bus sales see decrease of 70.3% during 2020's first seven months

The bus manufacturing industry in our country has also been hit by the pandemic, by registering the fact that wholesale sales of this type of units decreased 70.3% from January to July of this year, in comparison with the same period of last year. According to numbers published by INEGI in the Administrative Register of the Heavy Vehicles Automotive Industry, the sale of vehicles for passenger mobility is registering its lowest levels.

The Jaguar's 2021 F-Type update arrives to Mexico

The update of the Jaguar F-Type arrives to Mexico for the 2021 model; the 2021 Jaguar F-Type's range in Mexico is made up by three levels of equipment: R-Dynamic, First Edition and R. The engine offer is made up by three options. The P300 configuration, which is the entry option, is made up by a turbo 4 cylinder 2.0 liter engine, capable of generating 300 hp and 295 lb-ft. It's followed by the P380 version with a V6 supercharged 3.0 liter engine, which performs at 380 hp and 339 lb-ft. The most powerful version corresponds to the F-Type P575 R, with a V8 supercharged 5.0 liter engine, capable of generating 575 hp and 516 lb-ft.
