JAC enters the commercial vehicle market in Mexico

JAC Motors announced that as of November 1 it will begin selling its new line of commercial vehicles in Mexico, which in addition to offering models with turbo-diesel engines, will also sell all-electric units. According to the company, the new line of commercial vehicles will usher in the era of electrification of two 100% electric models that are part of its portfolio, the E Sunray and the E X350. The models with diesel engines will be the JAC Sunray, which will arrive in Mexico in two combustion versions in cargo and passenger options; the last mile model JAC X250 and the cargo model JAC X350.

MG Motor arrives to the Mexican market with three models

MG Motor, belonging to the Chinese group SAIC, made its official arrival to the Mexican market with three models. The first is the MG5, a compact sedan, while ZS and HS are the options in the SUV segment. SAIC Motor Mexico will open 17 MG agencies in our country before the end of the year. The first six will begin operating in Mexico City, Nuevo Leon, State of Mexico and Quintana Roo. The rest will open in November and December, and will be located in Puebla, Yucatan, Veracruz, Tabasco, San Luis Potosi and Coahuila.

KIA introduces the new 2021 KIA Rio

The KIA Rio is one of the South Korean brand's most important products in Mexico, and just months before the end of the year they present us with their update, a subtle update that seeks to keep it as one of the favorites in the segment. The novelty in the 2021 KIA Rio is the introduction of a new version called S Pack, with which comes an aesthetic sports kit, consisting of spoiler, skirt and diffuser. It retains 17-inch aluminum wheels. It maintains its 1.6-liter vacuum engine with 121 hp and 111 lb-ft of torque.

Jeep adds another limited edition to the family with the new Wrangler Edition Willys 2021

Another limited edition in the Jeep family arrives to Mexico, the new Wrangler Edition Willys 2021 that was inspired by the original Willys Overland CJ-3A, 1948 and is characterized mainly by combining components Trail Rated heavy duty, with details of classic design of the brand. Based on the Unlimited Sport S eTorque version, this model is limited to 100 units which are available in four colors with a three-panel black rigid canopy. The engine remains the same, a 3.6-liter V6 with 285 hp, 260 pound-feet of torque and an eight-speed automatic transmission.

Ford's Bronco Sport pre-sale begins

The Ford Bronco Sport range for Mexico includes four equipment levels: Big Bend, Outer Banks, Badlands and First Edition. The Ford Bronco Sport is available in Mexico with two mechanical configurations. The most accessible versions employ a 1.5-liter, three-cylinder turbo engine, capable of generating 181 hp and 190 lb-ft. The range stops replace it with a 2.0-liter four-cylinder turbo engine, which develops 245 hp and 275 lb-ft. It can be set aside with MXN$20,000 and deliveries of the first units are scheduled for January 2021. Its price range goes from MXN$618,000 to MXN$803,000.

Volvo Trucks releases the VNL 300 in our country

In order to expand its product range, Volvo Trucks is preparing to launch its VNL 300 vehicle with both compressed and liquefied natural gas. Julio Cesar Hernandez, commercial director of the company, said that for the implementation of this project the company has worked closely with Transportes Presurizados for the launch of the VNL powered by liquefied natural gas. As for the differences between both versions, the director commented that the liquefied natural gas version offers greater autonomy, practically twice that of the compressed natural gas version.

RAM launches 10th anniversary edition of the 1500 Longhorn

RAM is celebrating its 10th anniversary as an independent brand this year, so it is launching an edition paying tribute to the new 2021 1500 Longhorn 10th Anniversary Edition, a model that joins the range with special features. Specifically in this edition, it will have the Longhorn 10th Anniversary Edition emblem, the multifunction rear door and 20-inch wheels in various finishes. The new 2021 RAM 1500 Longhorn 10th Anniversary Edition will arrive in our country, but during the second quarter of 2021. Its price will be announced when its arrival date approaches.
