Mexico occupies second place in car exports to the United States

Mexico displaced Canada from second place in car exports to the United States for the first time in history. Likewise, if the current trend continues, Mexico is about to surpass Japan as leading vehicle provider in the American market. In 2019, Mexico exported vehicles to the northern country for a value of US$38,058 million, an increase of 8.1%; while Canada exports added up to US$37,819 million, a decrease of 1%, to annual rates.

Packaging Laboratory is opened in Guanajuato

The launch of the Packaging Laboratory in Silao, Guanajuato was carried out recently. Said project was undertaken between the State's Training Institute (IECA), the International Cooperation Agency of Japan (JICA) and the Japanese company Kanepackage. "There will be 600 trained people a year here, especially for companies that design and manufacture packaging locally, not only for the automotive sector but also for companies that work for the final consumer," explained Juan Carlos Lopez Rodriguez, general director of IECA Guanajuato.

Mitchell Plastics invests US$22 million in Queretaro

The Canadian capital company, Mitchell Plastics, announced the expansion of its first plant in Mexico. In only five years, the company which specializes in the injection and painting of plastic components for clients like Ford and General Motors, has around 600 employees. With this new expansion, for which US$22 million were invested, there's the expected creation of 255 new jobs. Currently there's 90 companies with Canadian capital like Mitchell Plastics in the state of Queretaro. The plant spans 12,000 square meters where they make interior and decorative components.

The Audi Q3 to Mexico in its second generation

A sportier look, greater habitability and versatility distinguish the new Audi Q3. Audi's new family SUV is available in three versions and two engine options. The second generation of the Q3 is available in our country starting at MXN$584,900. In its release to the Mexican market, Audi offers the Q3 with two TFSI engines, in combination with front-wheel drive or the quattro drive. Its maximum power goes from 150hp to 180hp.

Peugeot will debut the Peugeot Landtrek in Mexico

Mexico will be the first country to receive the new Peugeot Landtrek, whose release is foreseen for the year's second semester, as part of the company's strategy for commercial vehicles for Latin America and Africa. At the time of release there are two engine options. The first one is of 1.9 liters, capable of generating 150 hp and 258 lb-ft, available only with a manual six-speed transmission. The second option with a turbocharged 2.4 liter propeller, capable of producing 210 hp and 236 lb-ft. It can be coupled with a manual six speed transmission or an automatic one.

Faurecia obtains Top Employer certificate for the second time

Top Employers Institute certified Faurecia Mexico as a Top Employer 2020. Adrian Dingfelder, human resources director of Faurecia Mexico, indicated that they applied development programs, initiatives that promote gender diversity and talent enhancement, in the interest of collaborators advancing within the company. The survey of the best human resources practices has more than 100 questions that cover 600 practices of personal development distributed in 10 topics: talent strategies, planning of the workforce, talent acquisition, on boarding, learning and development, performance management, leadership development, career and succession management, benefits and compensation, as well as culture.

ZF in El Marques obtains "Quality plant of the year" award

The plant of the company TRW Braking Systems belonging to German corporation ZF (which began activities in 2015) obtained the award "Quality plant of the year 2019" by excelling among 43 other production sites of this division in the world. The following thanks to the processes and manufacturing programs followed in pro of quality and performance of the systems and products made here. The plant is specialized in the manufacturing of braking systems, for which it currently employs 805 people.
