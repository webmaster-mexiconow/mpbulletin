JAC bets on electrification in Mexico

JAC has decided to make a huge bet on the Mexican automotive industry, since besides presenting five electric models for our country, they also announced that they'll be assembled in Ciudad Sahagun, Hidalgo. The models are the sedan E-J4; the SUV family E-Sei1, E-Sei2 and E-Sei4; and the medium sized pickup E-Frison T8. The prices for the first three arriving products range from MXN$495,000 to $750,000 and have autonomy ranges going from 320 to 400 km.

BASF inaugurates automotive repainting training center in Toluca

Industrial group specialized in chemical products BASF, inaugurated the Automotive Repainting Training Center in Toluca, State of Mexico, which seeks to encourage the professionalization of automotive industry painters. The training center has an extension of 4,000 square meters, has six areas for practical training, so students can learn the Science of Colors, Repainting Systems and Business Administration and other skills.
