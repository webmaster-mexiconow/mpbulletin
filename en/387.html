Kia sold 7,819 units during November

Kia Motors Mexico is expected to end 2020 as one of the main brands at a national level, reaching 7,819 units delivered, a figure that places it in 4th position at the end of November, a goal set by the automaker in Mexico. Nissan, Volkswagen and Ford among the most searched on the Internet by Mexicans

Nissan, Volkswagen Group and Ford were the automotive companies that had the greatest reach with the total number of Internet users in Mexico because they were among the most sought after, according to information from Comscore on the Mexican automotive market on the Internet. In a conference, Ivan Marchant, Vice President of Mexico, Colombia, Peru and Central America for Comscore, stated that these three brands account for approximately 8% of Mexico's Internet users, surpassing General Motors, Honda, Hyundai, FCA, Kavak, Toyota and Renault.

Honda Fit bids farewell to Mexico with Final Edition

The Honda Fit will soon be discontinued in order to make room for other models of the brand that have better adapted to the needs and demands of consumers. Thus, as a limited edition and with an extra load of equipment, the Honda Fit Final Edition is being launched in Mexico. The power source for the Honda Fit Final Edition is the same 1.5-liter engine, capable of generating 130 hp and 114 lb-ft. It is associated with a CVT automatic transmission, shared with the Hit and Fun versions. The more accessible version can be configured with a six-speed manual transmission. Production of the farewell edition of the Honda Fit in Mexico is limited to 300 units, each priced at MXN$349,900.

November registers an increase of 1.4% in car production

312,184 automobiles were assembled in Mexico in November, 1.4% more than in the same period last year, the National Institute of Statistics and Geography (INEGI) reported Friday. This is the first increase reported for an eleventh month since 2017, although it is a smaller annual percentage variation than that of October of this year, where it increased by 8.8%. This increase was derived from a greater demand for cars abroad, as 287,703 cars were exported, 4.7% higher than that reported for the same period in 2019.

Ford of Mexico wins for second year the Environmental Award

Ford of Mexico is awarded for the second consecutive year with the Environmental Award at a North American level, thanks to the work of Alberto Aguayo and Rafael Curra, environmental engineers at the Chihuahua plant, who positioned themselves as first and finalist respectively of the award, with the projects of Environmental Wellness Community Approach and Skimmer Oil Separator. These were created to raise awareness of environmental impact and demonstrate that efforts can make a positive impact on the planet.
