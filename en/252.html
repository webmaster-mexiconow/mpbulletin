July 22th, 2019
Dina delivered 52 buses in Hermosillo

Dina delivered 52 new buses to the Corporate Administration of Hermosillo; the buses will be incorporated to the public transport service of the state's capital, which would make for a total of 154 new buses that would, in turn, strengthen the municipality's service, according to the Plan of Operative Transport. Of the delivered units, 29 are Runner10 buses and 20 are Zafiro Dina-Ayco. They are comfortable and safe units and have engines made by Cummins of Mexico and are equipped with air conditioner, plastic seats and an electronic sign.
Mitsubishi's hybrid Outland arrives in Mexico

The Outlander has a seven year unlimited mileage and bumper to bumper guarantee, Mitsubishi Motors will market the sport utility vehicle (SUV), Outlander PHEV (Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle), in two versions: the SE and Limited. Furthermore, it will be equipped with an Atkinson-cycle gasoline engine of 2.0L and 117 horsepower and two electric motors (one in front and one in the back) with a capacity of 12 kWh each, which translates to an autonomy of 54km when in completely electrical setting; its combined autonomy is of up to 600km.
Hino Motors Mexico introduces mobile workshops for preventive maintenance

Nozomu Harada, president and general director of Hino Motors Sales Mexico, introduced the mobile workshop, made up of 41 hybrid units that will, on a national level, be used to give preventive maintenance on site and thus, reduce the time that clients use for this concept. On par with this, a program for two technical advisers to work in a coordinated matter was developed. This was done to optimize costumer's time and unit maintenance, with the goal of having units on the road for as long as possible.
There aren't enough engineers in Queretaro

While there is an important demand in the local industry, which generates thousands of job openings a year, the automotive and auto parts sector in the state is facing a shortage of engineers in the operative areas, warned Renato Villaseñor Mendoza, president of the Automotive Cluster of Queretaro (CAQ). He also said that the shortage is of one to two years of placement, which is why the cluster is working with the industry and the universities to prepare young graduates as quickly as they can, so that they can join the workforce in the shortest amount of time possible.
Jeep Gladiator arrives in Mexico

Jeep announced that the new Jeep Gladiator will arrive in Mexico. The Jeep Gladiator is available in Mexico with only one engine option: the 3.6L Pentastar V6, which is capable of generating 258 horsepower and 260 pounds per foot. The propellant is associated to an eight-speed automatic transmission and includes a start and stop system. The only available version, Rubicon, includes an 8.4" UConnect infotainment, which is compatible with Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, a digital instrument panel, a rear camera, a forward-facing off-road camera, frontal collision alert with an autonomous emergency break, adaptive cruise control, and a smart key, amongst others.
Automotive Cluster Laguna will promote the region in Europe

The Automotive Cluster Laguna is preparing to promote the region (Gomez Palacio, Durango, and Torreon, Coahuila) as an investment destination in Europe and several regions of Mexico, the region offers qualified personnel, low work absenteeism and an average turnover of 2 to 3.5%, informed Jose Luis Hotema de Santiago, president of the cluster. "We're currently formalizing a commercial mission to Spain and Germany; it's complex because we have to match the schedules of the companies that we will be visiting, but we have set as a possible date the last week of October," he said.
DENSO will begin expansion in Coahuila

The expansion work for provider DENSO will begin at the beginning of August, and will generate 500 new jobs. DENSO is one of the top ten automotive providers in the world, and the first stage of their expansion will add 15 thousand square meters of operations and will generate 500 new jobs. For this expansion, DENSO has allocated approximately US$6.5 million. Furthermore, the plant is dedicated to the manufacturing of refrigeration components and air conditioner components for OEMs.
