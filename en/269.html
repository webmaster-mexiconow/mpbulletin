Continental Guadalajara Tijera obtains silver level of the Mexican Norm in Labor Equality and No discrimination

Continental's Guadalajara Tijera plant was distinguished with the silver level of the Mexican Norm in Labor Equality and No discrimination by the minister of Work and Social Security, Luisa María Alcalde Luján. Marco Galluzzi, vice president of Human Relations of Continental Mexico said: "In Mexico we already have a 21% of women in management positions. Planta Guadalajara Tijera is spearheading our initiatives on a national level."
The new MINI Clubman arrives to Mexico

The new MINI Clubman arrives to Mexico, the new model marks a milestone for the British brand by integrating to its portfolio the fastest and most powerful never before seen vehicle for use in the streets so far, the MINI John Cooper Works Clubman, which offers unmatched agile and sport driving conditions. Furthermore, there will be 14 limited First Edition units in Thunder Grey, with a Melting Silver hood and lines and mirror shells in red, available for sale in Mexico.
Nissan Kicks celebrates three years of production in Mexico

In 2016 Mexico became the first country in which Nissan Kicks was incorporated to the product offer of the Japanese automaker, and in a short time it has become one of the public's favorite cars, not only in the country, but in Latin America as a whole. "Since its inception, Nissan Kicks has been successful in the region, placing itself within the taste of Latin American consumers. We're very proud to celebrate Kick's third anniversary," commented Luis Alberto Perez Ettedgui, Marketing Director for Nissan Latin America.
SUVs take over Mexican exports

The automotive industry in Mexico increasingly exports more vans. From January to August 2019 the exports of this type of vehicles increased 3.7%, while sedan exports dropped 2.4%, according to numbers from the Mexican Association of the Automotive Industry (AMIA). The vans with the biggest export growth were Volkswagen's Tiguan with 20.3%, and General Motors' Equinox with 11.2%. In August the Equinox was the SUV with the biggest export volume with 16,310 units; it was followed by Jeep Compass with 12,709 units and Tiguan with 12,666 vehicles.
The new JAC Sei4 makes its arrival to Mexico

JAC announced the arrival to Mexico of the new Sei4 which complements the brand's SUV portfolio with the goal of offering different options to satisfy the needs of the Mexican market. JAC Sei4 will be available in two versions: Active as the entry level version and Connect as the top of the range version. "We're very happy with this launch, since the arrival of the JAC Sei4 to Mexico is the result of the great acceptance we've had from the Mexican market," commented Elias Massri, president and CEO of Giant Motors Latin America.
GM assures that production in Mexico will continue despite on-going US strike

General Motors' corporate stance in Mexico regarding the operations in its San Luis Potosi plant, following the strike in the United States, is one of expectation regarding the results of the brand's renegotiation with the United Automobile Workers (UAW), the company's majority union in the US. Manuel Avila Fonseca, coordinator of Corporate Communication, affirmed that they have enough spare parts stock to keep assembling cars.
Daimler Trucks gives Masters of Quality award to Sisamex and Meritor Mexico

The Meritor Mexico and Automotive Systems of Mexico (Sisamex) plants received the Masters of Quality 2018 award from Daimler Trucks, which the factories that produce axels and brakes have obtained 10 times now. Because of this, the directives of both companies exalted their staff's work as a cause of the obtainment of such an award from the German corporation and its North American subsidiaries.
General Motors will incorporate Alexa to its vehicles

General Motors (GM) announced on Wednesday that it will integrate Amazon's Alexa to its vehicles beginning in the first quarter of 2020. In a press release, the company said that by taking advantage of 4G LTE connectivity already integrated in the vehicles and the upgradeable apps of the GM brand, Alexa will be available in 2018 model year and posterior vehicles as long as they have a compatible infotainment system. "This will also allow GM to create personalized abilities in the future, that are relevant for the client's vehicle," said the firm.
Camiones Metropolitanos GR, a distributor for MAN Truck & Bus Mexico is inaugurated

The opening of Camiones Metropolitanos GR took place, the distributor for MAN Truck & Bus Mexico is located to the south of Mexico City, and will provide clients with different services and the brand's product portfolio. Fernando Ruano, general director of Grupo Ruano and of the new distributor, informed that thanks to MAN Truck & Bus Mexico's support and to Mexico City's carriers, the opening of this distributor is "a dream come true" which will allow them to be close to carriers.
Daimler Financial Services Mexico names new president and general director

Starting next December, Gerhard Gross, which is currently working as vice president of Operations of Daimler Mobility (DMO) on a global level, will assume the Presidency and General Direction of Daimler Financial Services Mexico. The change comes because on September 30 Victor Calderon will leave the company, due to personal reasons, after 15 years of a successful career, during which he exhibited his commitment, loyalty and experience. Miguel Carrion, commercial and marketing director of Daimler Financial Services Mexico will serve as interim general director.
