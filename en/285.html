Service is an integral part of KIA's sales strategy

Before the complicated situation of the Mexican automotive market, a key factor to win and keep clients will be service, said Victor Aleman Soto, Public Relations Manager of the KIA Pesqueria plant. "The Mexican automotive sector is registering a drop in sales, fortunately KIA models, not so much. We believe that the way to regain or keep the consumer's preference will be through service, if we give a good service that client will come back and will see about renewing or buying a car that's from the same brand, and this is the challenge that we'll have through 2020," explained Aleman Soto.
Fiat will bring the Argo to Mexico

FCA announced that during the first few months of 2020 they will launch the Fiat Argo in Mexico, a subcompact hatchback that will seek to place itself against the Toyota Yaris, KIA Rio and VW Polo, among others. For Mexico the most likely offer is the one with a 1.3 liter engine with 109 hp and 103 lb-ft torque with a five speed manual transmission or an automatic one. We expect it to have ABS brakes, traction and stability control, and at least four air bags, although it would be nice to see six like in the SEAT Ibiza and KIA Rio.
Mexican auto part manufacturers should bet on the development of interiors and electronic sensors for electric vehicles: Deloitte

Auto parts manufacturers should plan ahead for the increase in the demand of hybrid and electric vehicles making investments to manufacture components and this type of technology, according to a study made by Deloitte. Alberto Torrijos, leading partner of Deloitte Automotive, said that Mexican auto parts manufacturers have a bigger potential in the development of interiors for hybrid and electric vehicles, and in electronic sensors, since the development of batteries is concentrated in China, United States and Europe.
Volkswagen Silao is a social responsibility benchmark

Preserving and rescuing the environment helps everyone, said the governor of Guanajuato, Diego Sinhue Rodriguez Vallejo, in his visit to the Volkswagen Silao plant, where he got to know the achievements and use expectations for the use of renewable energies, carried out by the automotive company. With the Volkswagen initiative, he added, there's evidence of the social awareness that socially responsible companies have towards their environment, and that it's not only about generating jobs and profit, but also about preserving and rescuing an environment that benefits everyone.

TIP Mexico celebrated its 26th anniversary

For its 26th anniversary TIP Mexico, leasing company for transport vehicles, cars and fleet administration, celebrated its 7th Invitational, event in which it reunited more than 180 executives from different industries. During the press conference previous to the event, they announced their double digit sustained growth numbers, which have consolidated their marker participation despite the hardships experienced by the sector.
CUPRA inaugurates its first flagship store in Mexico City

CUPRA is starting its engine in Mexico. As part of its growth strategy, the brand is premiering in Mexico with the opening of its first worldwide flagship store and with the release of the CUPRA Ateca. CUPRA has chosen Mexico City to open the first CUPRA Garage in the world, an exclusive space dedicated to the brand where car lovers will be able to live a unique experience. At the same time, the brand has opened six CUPRA Corners through the distributor network of Seat Mexico.
KIA Motors Mexico revealed its 2019 achievements

The KIA Motors Mexico plant located in Pesqueria presented the brand's achievements in production, sales and social responsibility so far this year. During the January to October 2019 period, KIA Motors Mexico has manufactured 244,800 units in the Pesqueria plant. KIA's total sales for 2019 mean an increase of 1.6% when compared to the first 10 months of last year. Corporate Social Responsibility is a fundamental part of KIA Motors Mexico's strategy, which is why there was a continuation of precious projects and the creation of new ones in key areas.
The Peugeot 2008 and 2018 will be available in Mexico

The French company will sell, for the first time, more than 10,000 units in Mexico by the end of 2019. At the end of 2020 the brand's Mexican distributors will see the arrival of the renewed Peugeot 208. Among the brand's new releases for our country we can highlight the new generation of the SUV 2008, which is completely renewed and will be available here soon, practically at the same time of its release in Europe slated for January of the coming year.
Transportes AMZ incorporated 30 Volkswagen 8.160 OD Minibus

A total of thirty 8.160 OD Minibus buses from Volkswagen Trucks and Buses, equipped with a EURO V engine and AYCO bodywork were delivered to the company Transportes AMZ in the State of Mexico, in presence of the state's minister of Mobility, Raymundo Martinez Carvajal. The allocation of this units was finalized by Miguel Angel Vallejo Alvarez, commercial director of MAN Truck & Bus Mexico, to Jose Manuel Gutierrez Hernandez, representative of Transportes AMZ.
