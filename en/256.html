JeffreyGroup is the new corporate communication and public relations agency for BMW Latin America

BMW Group Latin America selected JeffreyGroup Mexico like its new corpotate communication and public relations agency; this with the aim to have support in the implementation of the strategies in these areas for their three brands BMW, MINI and BMW Motorrad in the Latin American region. As of August 1st, the specialized firm with presence in Latin America will be the one in charge to support the corporate communication department in the management of their activities.
MAN Truck & Bus Mexico presents its new Robust line

The commercial Volkswagen brand expanded their product portfolio recently, with the launch of the Robust in its 15 and 17 tons models. Robust is directed to the medium duty segment of 15 and 17 tons, class 7 and 8, tending to the market niches like waste collection, cold boxes, dry boxes, and soda boxes. This new product line is equipped with MAN motors, developed with the Workline equipment baseline and using the baseline design of the Constellation cabin, thus keeping its robustness, agility in urban centers and low operational cost.
The regularization of chocolate cars would be damaging for the domestic market

The Mexican automotive industry asked President Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador to not regularize imported used vehicles, named chocolate cars, in the northern border, since this would exacerbate the sales crisis in the domestic market. "The last regularization of this type of vehicles executed by President Vicente Fox caused a sales decrease of up to 30%," recalled Guillermo Rosales, assistant general director of the Mexican Association of Automotive Distributors (AMDA).
BMW expects half its portfolio to have at least one hybrid version for 2020

The German automaker BMW wants to boost the sale of low emission vehicles in Mexico, which is why it expects that in 2020 half of its portfolio will have at least a hybrid version. In an interview, Maru Escobedo, general director of the brand in the country, noted that this is part of their 2023 strategy where the brand promised that each one of their vehicles world have the option to be electric or hybrid on a global level. Currently 6% of the total sales volume by BMW in Mexico for the first semester of 2019 is hybrid.
Car sales drop 7.9% in July

In July 2019 105,699 units were sold in Mexico, a 7.9% decrease compared to the same period of last year, as reported by the National Institute of Statistics and Geography (INEGI). This is the biggest reduction reported by the automotive industry for the seventh month of the year since 2009, when the global economic crisis hit. Guillermo Rosales, assistant general director of the Mexican Association of Automotive Distributors (AMDA), said that the negative factors that contribute to the automotive market deepen: debt and low purchase capacity of Mexican consumers and the distrust before the country's economic and political landscape.
Second generation of apprentices conclude training program in Audi Mexico

Audi Mexico celebrated the graduation of the second generation of apprentices, who finished the Dual Formation Program this year. This Audi Mexico program is based in the German dual education system, which combines theoretical academic knowledge with practical experience in a company. This year 77 apprentices concluded their formation in Audi Mexico. After successfully concluding the formation program, every student receives a job offer. In this concluding generation, 100% of the graduates will be hired by Audi Mexico. Additionally, 32% of the graduates are women.
Mercedes-Benz Buses delivers units to Estrella Roja Puebla

Mercedes-Benz Buses and Camiones Rivera, in conjunction with Estrella Roja of Puebla, will mobilize tourism in the city that's the cradle of Mexican gastronomy, Puebla. The delivered units will service the foreign route of Puebla State to Mexico City. They are Paradiso 1200 buses with an OC500 RF 1943 chassis, they have last generation BlueTec5 low contaminant emission engines and have the SafetyBus security system.
